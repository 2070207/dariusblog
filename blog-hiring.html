Now Hiring!

Dimitri  |  13.04.2024 09:45  |  10 comments


Introduction: We are excited to announce that Darius’ Baked Goods is growing and we’re looking for individuals to join our team! We believe in the power of fresh ingredients, great minds, and excellent communication, and we’re looking for people who share our passion for excellence.

Vacant Positions: We have several positions open for the roles of Web Designer, Customer Server, and Baker. Whether you’re a passionate website designer, a socializer who loves interacting with customers, or a culinary enthusiast with a love for baking, we have a place for you in our team.

Required Skills: For all positions, we value teamwork, creativity, and a strong work ethic. Our Web Designers should have a great understanding of UI/UX principles, proficiency in design software and is able to recieve constructive criticism. Our Customer Servers should have excellent communication skills and a customer-first mindset. Our Bakers should have a passion for baking, knowledge of various baking techniques, and follow food safety regulations.

How to Apply: If you’re ready to take the next step in your career and join a team that’s passionate about quality and creativity, we’d love to hear from you! Please Contact Us on our blogpage to get in touch and submit your application. We can’t wait to welcome you to the Darius’ Baked Goods group!
